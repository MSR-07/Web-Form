Validating Web Froms

Firstname must be alphanumeric and contain 3 - 16 characters

Lastname must be alphanumeric and contain 3 - 16 characters

Email must be a valid address, e.g. example@example.com

Password must alphanumeric (@, _ and - are also allowed) and
be 8 - 20 characters

A valid Telephone number (11 digits and xxx-xxx-xxxx)

Bio must contain only lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens and
be 8 - 50 characters
